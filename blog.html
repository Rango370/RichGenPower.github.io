Generator Tips & Safety

Maintenance Checklist

  1. Inspect oil level and change per schedule
  2. Check coolant and belts
  3. Test under load monthly
  4. Replace fuel filters
  5. Log runtime hours

Safety Precautions

  • Operate in well-ventilated areas
  • Keep away from flammable materials
  • Use grounded outlets and proper cabling
  • Never backfeed into utility without transfer switch
